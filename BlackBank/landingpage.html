Banking Service

BlackBank is a Bitcoin banking service. We specialize in handling, transferring, and the storing of Bitcoins. The concept of the service is to improve security and efficiency of a transaction. Whenever a direct Bitcoin transaction is made, there is a time frame in which the amount is transferred, followed by a minimum number of confirmations required to be secure. As a result, whenever there is a direct transfer, the time required becomes inefficient.

BlackBank functions as a banking service, where Bitcoins are first deposited and confirmed by our servers. Upon confirmation, the Bitcoins can be virtually transferred from one BlackBank account to another BlackBank account instantly. At any point after, the account holders can then withdraw their account balance to the Bitcoin address in their profile.

BlackBank also provides statements for accounts that are viewable in the same manner as traditional banking statements. This provides a more manageable way to view and organize your transactions. The only difference is that we hold no personal information and all outgoing and incoming transactions are done through the pseudonyms of usernames.

The transfer of larger balances into a BlackBank account also allows for smaller transactions with other account holders. Bitcoin structure was originally designed where small transactions could end up being cost in-efficient; this issue is circumvented as larger amounts are first deposited into your BlackBank account and then transactions between other users are through the server rather than through Bitcoin.

As the amount is transferred virtually on our server, the transfer fee is a low-flat rate determined by the current Bitcoin exchange value of $0.01 USD + 2.00% and withdrawal fee at 0.0001 + 2.00% BTC . There are no deposit fees except the ones incurred by Bitcoin. Rate changes will always be announced three months in advance.
